Game Info

Controls:
Arrow Keys → Move
Space → Jump
D → Throw bottle

Objective:
Collect coins and bottles, defeat enemies, and beat the final boss.

Tips:
Jump on chickens to defeat them.
Use bottles strategically against the boss.
Keep an eye on your health.

Good luck and have fun!
